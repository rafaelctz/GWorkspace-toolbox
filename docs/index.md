# GWorkspace Toolbox

Welcome to GWorkspace Toolbox - a powerful suite of tools designed for Google Workspace administrators in schools and educational institutions to streamline daily operations and management tasks.

## Overview

GWorkspace Toolbox provides a modern, user-friendly interface for managing Google Workspace operations that would otherwise require complex scripts or manual processes. Built specifically for educational administrators, it helps schools save time and reduce errors when managing students, faculty, and staff accounts.

## Key Features

### Alias Extractor
Export user aliases from your Google Workspace domain to CSV format for auditing, reporting, or migration purposes.

![Alias Extractor Interface](/screenshots/alias-extractor.png)

### Attribute Injector
Batch inject custom attributes to Organizational Units (OUs), making it easy to manage user properties across your domain.

![Attribute Injector Interface](/screenshots/attribute-injector.png)

### OU Group Sync
Automatically synchronize users from Organizational Units to Google Groups with smart sync capabilities and scheduling options.

![OU Group Sync Interface](/screenshots/ou-group-sync.png)

## Why GWorkspace Toolbox?

- **Easy to Use**: Clean, intuitive interface requiring no technical expertise
- **Multi-Language**: Full support for English, Spanish, and Portuguese
- **Secure**: OAuth 2.0 authentication with your Google Workspace account
- **Open Source**: Free and community-driven development
- **Self-Hosted**: Run on your own infrastructure for complete data control
- **Automatic Updates**: Docker deployment with Watchtower keeps you up to date
